About Company

Welcome to Hotel Singhs - Your Premier Boutique Hotel in Vashi Navi Mumbai

Welcome to Hotel Singhs, the premier boutique hotel in Vashi Navi Mumbai, where luxury and comfort converge in the heart of the city. Our mission is to provide a seamless and unforgettable experience for every guest, with a wide array of amenities designed for your utmost comfort and enjoyment.

From hassle-free parking to 24/7 check-in, our friendly staff is here to greet you with warmth and professionalism. Enjoy the cool ambiance provided by our top-notch air conditioning, indulge in a sumptuous breakfast to start your day right, and stay entertained with in-room televisions offering a variety of channels. For your peace of mind, each room comes equipped with a safe to secure your valuables.

Our amenities are tailored to meet all your needs, ensuring a comfortable and delightful stay. Recharge at our coffee bar with premium blends, stay connected with high-speed wifi, and make use of our well-equipped workspaces. Our elevator provides easy access to all floors, accommodating guests of all mobilities.

With dedicated room service available around the clock, we are committed to exceeding your expectations and creating cherished memories during your stay. Discover a new level of luxury and hospitality at our boutique hotel – your exquisite home away from home.
